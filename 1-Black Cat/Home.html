About the Black Cat

A black cat, or black cat, is a domestic cat with melanism, which gives it the dark coloring of its coat. Due to myths propagated in the medieval era, the belief that black cats were witches turned into animals was spread. Therefore, in part of the western world there is a myth that says that crossing a black cat is a sign of bad omen. However, in several other cultures black cats are revered, being associated with the presence of good luck.International institutions for the protection of animals have established that on October 27th, the day of the black cat is celebrated, a date created with the aim of publicizing their qualities and encouraging the adoption of these felines, since these animals are usually less adopted than the specimens. of other colors.
